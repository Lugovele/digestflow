# Product UX Naming

DigestFlow may still appear as the internal project, repo, and backend naming surface.

For product-facing UX copy, use **PostFlow** as the primary name and prefer language centered on:

- post ideas
- research sources
- post drafts
- publish-ready posts
- workspace

When choosing user-facing labels, favor post-generation language over internal digest/topic terminology unless a backend or diagnostics label must remain unchanged for technical clarity.
